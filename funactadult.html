Fun Activities for Adults with Intellectual Disabilities

Activity 1: Art and Crafts

Engage in creative art and craft activities to foster creativity and fine motor skills. Use simple materials like paper, glue, and colors.

Activity 2: Music and Dance

Enjoy music and dance sessions to improve physical coordination and enjoy the rhythm. Play favorite songs and encourage movement.

Activity 3: Outdoor Games

Participate in outdoor games like ball toss, relay races, or simple exercises to enhance physical fitness and teamwork.
