Fun Activities for Children with Intellectual Disabilities

Welcome

Discover a variety of engaging and enjoyable activities designed specifically for children with intellectual disabilities. These activities promote learning, social interaction, and most importantly, fun!

Activity Highlights

Music Therapy

Experience the joy of music through interactive sessions that encourage self-expression and creativity.

Art and Craft

Unleash your creativity with fun art and craft projects designed to enhance motor skills and imagination.

Outdoor Games

Join in on a variety of outdoor games that promote physical activity and teamwork in a safe and supportive environment.

Story Time

Enjoy captivating stories that foster a love for reading and improve listening skills and comprehension.
